The 2013 Recommender Systems Challenge is associated with ACM RecSys 2013. Known as RecS ys Challenge 2013, this is a LBS contest organized by Yelp. The theme of this year X  X  competition is personalized business recommendations for Yelp users. We provide a dataset on over 10,000 businesses, eight thousand check-in sites, 40 thousand users and 200 thousand reviews. Contestants are asked to predict the users X  future ratings on businesses. The competition starts on May 3, 2013 and ends on August 31, 2013. The leaderboard for the competition is open and more detailed information is available at h ttps://www.kaggle.com/c/yelp-recsys-2013. A $500 prize will be presented to the winners at the RecSys 2013 conference. After the competition closes, we will hold a full-day workshop, co-located with the ACM RecSys 2013 conference, to discuss interesting approaches to the competition problem as well as lessons learned. We invite all contest participants to present their approach to building business recommendations. Contributions may focus on any individual step such as feature extraction or model building; or describe an end-to-end system to predict business ratings. Submissions are welcome in the form of extended abstracts of proposed presentations. These ex tended abstract should give overviews on the approaches us ed in the competition problem. One submission type is accepted: short manuscripts of 2 to 4 pages. The extended abstracts will be evaluated for originality, contribution significance, and ove rall quality. Submissions should be in the form of a PDF attach ment to a contest submission on Kaggle on August 31, 2013. Accepted speakers will be invited to submit a short paper that describes their approach and results. The short papers will be distributed at the workshop and made available online afterwards.
