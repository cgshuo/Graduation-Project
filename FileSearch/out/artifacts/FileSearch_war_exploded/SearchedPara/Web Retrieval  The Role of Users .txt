 H.3.3 [Information Search and Retrieval] Algorithms, Human Factors Web Retrieval, User Interaction Ricardo Baeza-Yates is VP of Yahoo! Research for Europe and Latin America. Until 2005, he was the director of the Center for Web Research at the Department of Computer Science of the Engineering School of the Univ ersity of Chile, and ICREA Professor at the Dept. of Technology of University Pompeu Fabra in Barcelona, Spain. He is co-author of the book Modern Information Retrieval , whose 2 nd edition appeared in 2011, as Algorithms and Data Structures , Addison-Wesley, 1991; and co-editor of Information Retrieval: Algorithms and Data Structures, Prentice-Hall, 1992, among more than 200 other publications. He has been PC Chair of the most important conferences in the field of Web Search and Web Mining. Most recently, he was one of the co-chairs WWW X 2010 Web Search Track and PC co-chair of SIGIR'2011. He has received the organization of American States award for young researchers in exact sciences (1993) and with tw o Brazilian res earch articles (1997). In 2003 he was the first com puter scientist to be elected to the Chilean Academy of Sc iences. During 2007, he was awarded the Graham Medal for innovation in computing, given by the University of Waterloo to distinguished ex-alumni and in 2009 was named ACM Fellow. Yoelle Maarek is a Senior Research Director at Yahoo! Labs and heads Yahoo! Research in Israel since 2009. Prior to this, Yoelle was the Director of Google Haif a Engineering Center, which she opened in 2006. There, she led the development of "Google Suggest", a query completion feature deployed on google.com and YouTube worldwide. Before this, Yoelle had been with IBM Research, first in the US, and then in Israel, where she led the search and collaboration department and became a Distinguished Engineer. She gra duated from the ENPC in Paris, France, and received her DEA in CS from Paris VI University. Yoelle obtained her PhD in Computer Science from Technion, Israel Institute of Technology. Yoelle has published extensively in the Web and IR communities. Most recently, she has served as Program co-chair of WWW'2009, as senior PC member at several SIGIR and WWW conferences. She is also Program co-chair for WSDM X 2012 and SIGIR X 2012. Yoelle is a member of the Board of Governors of Technion, and was appointed ACM Distinguished Scientist in 2010.
