H.3.3 [Information Search and Retrieval] Algorithms, Human Factors Web Retrieval, User Interaction Ricardo Baeza-Yates is VP of Yahoo! Research for Europe and Latin America, leading the labs at Barcelona, Spain and Santiago, Chile. Until 2005, he was director of the Center for Web Research at the CS department of the University of Chile, and ICREA Professor at the Dept. of ICT of University Pompeu Fabra in Barcelona. He is co-author of the book Modern Information Retrieval, whose second edition will appear in 2010, among more than 200 publications. He has received the organization of American States award for young researchers in exact sciences (1993) and the CLEI Latin American distinction for CS (2009). In 2003 he was the first computer scien tist to be elected to the Chilean Academy of Sciences and in 2009 was named ACM Fellow. Yoelle Maarek is the Senior Director of Yahoo! Research in Haifa, Israel since 2009. Prior to this, Yoelle was the Director of Google Haifa Engineering Center, which she opened in 2006. There, she led the development of "Google Suggest", a query completion feature deployed on google.com and YouTube worldwide. From 1989 until 2006, Yoelle was with IBM Research, first in the US, and then in Israel, where she led the search and collaboration department and became a Distinguished Engineer. She graduated from the ENPC in Paris, France, and received her DEA in CS from Paris VI University, both in 1985. Yoelle obtained her PhD in CS from Technion in 1989. She has served as co-chair of the technical program of WWW'2009, and as seni or PC member at several SIGIR and WWW conferences. Yoelle is also a member of the Board of Directors of the Caesarea-Rothschild Institute at Haifa University and of the Board of Governors of the Technion.
