 I will discuss the use of graphical models for data mining. I will review key research areas including structure learning, variational methods, a relational modeling, and describe ap-plications ranging from web traffic analysis to AIDS vaccine design.
 David Heckerman is founder and manager of the Machine Learning and Applied Statistics Group at Microsoft Re-search. Since 1992, he has been a Senior Researcher at Microsoft, where he has created applications including data-mining tools in SQL Server and Commerce Server, the junk-mail filter in Outlook, Hotmail, and MSN, handwriting recog-nition in the Tablet PC, text mining software in Sharepoint Portal Server, troubleshooters in Windows, and the Answer Wizard in Office. David received his Ph.D. and M.D. from Stanford University and is a AAAI Fellow.
